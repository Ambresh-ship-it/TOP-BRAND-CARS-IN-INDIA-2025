CAR COMPANY BRANDS

TOYOTA

Toyota Motor Corporation is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer known for its reliability and durability, a wide range of vehicles (from hatchbacks to large SUVs), and pioneering hybrid technology.

BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke, or Bavarian Motor Works in English) is a German company that is one of the world's leading manufacturers of premium automobiles and motorcycles. Known for performance and luxury, BMW is headquartered in Munich, Germany, and is part of the "German Big 3" luxury automakers alongside Audi and Mercedes-Benz.

TATA

Tata offers a wide range of cars, including popular models like the Nexon, Harrier, and Punch, across various body styles (SUV, hatchback, sedan) and fuel types (petrol, diesel, CNG, and electric). The company also has several upcoming models, such as the Sierra EV and the 2025 Punch, and is known for its focus on safety and design. The current lineup features popular and stylish vehicles, with prices for their new models ranging from the entry-level Tiago at ₹4.57 Lakh to the high-end Harrier EV starting at ₹21.49 Lakh.

MARUTI SUZUKI

Maruti Suzuki is a major carmaker in India known for its wide range of affordable and fuel-efficient vehicles, including popular models like the Swift, Baleno, Brezza, and Ertiga. The company offers cars through its Arena and Nexa dealerships, and provides options in petrol, CNG, and hybrid fuel types. Key features include a vast service network, technology like Smart Hybrid and Suzuki Connect, and a focus on safety through features like specially designed headrests.

HONDAI

Hyundai offers a wide range of vehicles including SUVs, sedans, and hatchbacks, with models like the Creta, Verna, and Exter being popular choices. The company provides cars with various fuel types, such as petrol, diesel, CNG, and electric, and has a global presence with manufacturing facilities in India and exports to numerous countries.
